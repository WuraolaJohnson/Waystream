Frequently asked questions

Waystream is an audio streaming platform that provides creators and listeners with the best audio streaming experience. With Waystream, you can broadcast different kinds of audio content to the world with just a few clicks; listeners also enjoy access to diverse types of quality audio content from creators.

You can use Waystream to live broadcast audio, upload and share podcasts, broadcast virtual events, talk shows, or seminars, and share diverse kinds of audio content. As a listener, you can also listen to various audio content ranging from church sermons to talk shows, concerts, live events, podcasts and so much more.

To broadcast on Waystream you need to download and install the Waystream desktop app at https://waystream.io/download.

Waystream is free to use for at most 30 minutes daily; for additional talk time, you’ll be required to subscribe to a paid plan.

To subscribe to a paid plan of your choice, follow the steps below: 1. Install the Waystream desktop app. 2. Click on Billing on the nav bar. 3. Click on “Upgrade plan” to view the different available plans and select your preferred plan. 4. Click on “Proceed to pay” and fill in your payment details. 5. Upon confirmation of payment, your plan will automatically be upgraded to your paid plan.

Waystream has different subscription plans to suit your preference. To find out more about the different subscription plans available, kindly visit https://waystream.io/pricing.

If you run out of talk time before your renewal date, you can subscribe to another plan of your choice to get additional talk time.

Upon signing up, you gain access to a 14-day free trial period to broadcast without limits on Waystream. After your first 14 days free trial, your account is changed to the forever free plan with access to 30 minutes of free talk time daily.

Waystream serves as a tool for broadcasters to live broadcast audio content, interact with their audience in real-time, and deliver quality audio to their audience worldwide.
